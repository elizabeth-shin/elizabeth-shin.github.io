# AI-Augmented Engineering Certification

This blueprint defines a governance-first, capability-based certification system designed for engineering and product organizations adopting AI at scale.

---

## Core components

- **Model architecture & governance patterns**  
- **Capability maps for engineering teams**  
- **Assessment logic and progression pathways**  
- **Implementation playbooks and workflow design**  

---

## Purpose

The certification system is designed to:

- Accelerate AI-native habits  
- Reduce cognitive load through structured, in-flow learning  
- Align engineering practices with organizational governance  
- Provide measurable adoption and capability uplift  

More detailed artifacts will be published as they are finalized.
